There are five main roles in League of Legends:

  • Top Lane
  • Jungle
  • Mid Lane
  • ADC (Attack Damage Carry)
  • Support

Each role has its own unique responsibilities:

  • Top Lane: Usually plays tanks or bruisers, and focuses on controlling the top side of the map.
  • Jungle: Roams the map, clears jungle camps, and helps control objectives like Dragon and Baron Nashor.
  • Mid Lane: Often plays mages or assassins, and focuses on controlling the middle of the map and roaming to other lanes.
  • ADC: Deals ranged physical damage and relies on a support to protect them during team fights.
  • Support: Helps the team in a variety of ways, such as providing vision, healing or shielding allies, and initiating fights or peeling for the ADC.
